What we are about!

ShadowBall Co. is a new and growing business built on a love for all things Pokémon. From cute favorites to rare and powerful finds, we're passionate about collecting and sharing the excitement of opening booster packs, Elite Trainer Boxes, and other Pokémon TCG products. We sell a variety of these items so trainers and collectors alike can experience the same joy and spark of discovery with every pack!
